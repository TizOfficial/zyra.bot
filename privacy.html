Privacy

As of: June 11, 2025


The protection of your personal data is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information we collect in connection with your use of the Zyra Discord bot and how we use it.


1. Collection and use of data

Zyra stores certain server data to provide its features. This includes:

  • Server ID and channel IDs (for configurations such as welcome messages and ticket systems).
  • User IDs (e.g., for leveling systems, moderation logs, and ticket assignments).
  • Moderation logs (penalties such as bans, kicks, and mutes - these can be stored for a limited time to comply with Discord's rules).
    • .
  • Bot configuration settings per server.

Zyra does not collect or store personal messages or voice channel content. We do not access private conversations.

2. Purpose of data processing

The data collected is used exclusively to ensure and improve Zyra's functionality. This includes providing moderation tools, managing tickets, implementing the leveling system, and storing server-specific settings.

3. Sharing of data

We do not share your data or your server's data with third parties unless necessary to fulfill the bot's functions (e.g., interacting with the Discord API) or required by law. We do not sell or rent data.

4. Data security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect the data we store from loss, destruction, access, alteration, and distribution by unauthorized persons. The data is stored on secure servers.

5. Your rights

You have the right to receive information about the data stored about you or your server, to request its correction or deletion, as well as to restrict or object to processing. Please contact us using the email address provided in the Imprint

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified from time to time to comply with legal requirements or changes to our services. The most current version will always be available on this page. We recommend that you review this policy regularly.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about data protection, please contact us at the email address provided in the Imprint.
